PAS24 Security Certification

PAS24 is the UK's leading security standard for windows and doors, ensuring your home is protected against break-in attempts. Our products undergo rigorous testing to meet the highest security requirements.

What PAS24 Guarantees:

  • Resistance to Forced Entry - Tested against common burglary methods including mechanical attacks and impacts
  • Building Regulations Compliance - Required for new builds under Approved Document Q
  • Mechanical Testing - Products withstand forces up to 450kg applied to locks and hardware
  • Manual Attack Tests - Simulates intruder attempts using tools like screwdrivers and crowbars
  • Impact Resistance - Tested with weighted rams to ensure structural integrity

Your Benefits:

  • Enhanced Home Security - Proven protection against opportunistic burglars and break-in attempts
  • Insurance Advantages - Many insurers offer reduced premiums for PAS24 certified installations
  • Quality Assurance - Products are built to last and tested by UKAS-accredited laboratories
  • Legal Compliance - Essential for new residential properties in England and Wales
  • Peace of Mind - Independent certification guarantees security performance

FENSA Certification

FENSA (Fenestration Self-Assessment Scheme) is a government-authorised scheme ensuring compliance with Building Regulations for all window and door installations. Every Skylon installation comes with full FENSA certification.

What FENSA Covers:

  • Building Regulations Compliance - Full adherence to UK Building Regulations for thermal performance and safety
  • Local Council Registration - Your installation is automatically registered with your local authority
  • Energy Efficiency - Certified thermal performance reduces utility costs and environmental impact
  • Insurance-Backed Warranty - Your warranty is protected by insurance for complete peace of mind
  • Professional Installation - Only FENSA-registered installers can issue certificates

Why FENSA Matters:

  • Legal Requirement - Mandatory for replacement windows and doors installed after April 2002
