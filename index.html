About Skylon

Welcome to Skylon Timber & Glazing, where craftsmanship meets functionality. We specialize in creating high-quality timber windows that enhance both the aesthetic appeal and energy efficiency of your home.

Traditional Sash Windows

Our expertise lies in creating authentic Georgian, Victorian, and Edwardian sash windows that perfectly match London's period architecture. Each window is meticulously crafted to preserve historical aesthetics while incorporating modern performance standards.

Whether your property is in a Conservation Area or is a Listed Building, we understand the planning requirements and deliver windows that meet both heritage and building regulation standards.

Why Choose Skylon

  • Premium Timber - Hardwood, Accoya & Oak - Painted windows are exclusively made from hardwood. Upon request, we can produce from Accoya or oak. We manufacture using engineered timber, which prevents warping over time. Guaranteed durability and weather resistance, perfect for London's climate. Our timber is sourced from sustainable forests.
  • Premium Spray Finishing System - Timber is immersed in preprimer, then primed twice with primer, and finally topped with a finish coat. We use only products from the finest manufacturers: Sikkens Wood Coatings and Teknos.
  • Modern Performance, Hidden Aesthetics - Slimline double glazing and draught-proofing that maintain the historic look while reducing noise and heat loss. All our windows and doors are PAS24 certified for enhanced security and FENSA registered for Building Regulations compliance.
  • Conservation Area Expertise - We design windows compliant with conservation requirements and Listed Buildings regulations, ensuring your property meets all planning standards.
  • In-House Workshop & FENSA Installation - Every window is handcrafted in our workshop near London and installed by our FENSA-certified installers, guaranteeing quality from production to completion.
  • Comprehensive Warranty & After-Sales Support - Peace of mind with our insurance-backed warranty and dedicated customer service team.
  • Part of Skylon Group - As part of the Skylon Group, we can provide bespoke solutions for your entire window surroundings, including comprehensive construction services tailored to your project needs.

Handcrafted Excellence

Every Skylon window is handcrafted in our workshop near London by skilled artisans who understand the intricacies of traditional joinery. We use time-honoured techniques combined with modern precision to create windows that will last for generations.
